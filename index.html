🚨 Key Rules & Dependencies

• Pre-emergent vs. Seed: You cannot apply crabgrass pre-emergent and reseed in the same season. Pick one.
• Herbicide heat limit: No broadleaf herbicide when temps exceed 85–90°F.
• Seeding cutoff: October 15 — absolute last date for fall seeding.
• Mowing rule: Never cut more than ⅓ of blade length at once.
• Fertilizer on dry grass: Spread when blades are dry, then water in. Avoid early AM / late PM.
• Soil test every 3–4 years: Drives fertilizer rates and lime decisions.
